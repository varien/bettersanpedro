# BetterSanPedro.ph - Tourism Research

Last researched: 2026-07-21

The tourism page now publishes real San Pedro attraction leads instead of an empty directory. Each record carries a status:

- Official source: a city or responsible-agency source directly supports the record.
- Historical / archived: the source documents a past event or project; it is not a current schedule.
- Research lead - verify: a government planning reference or reputable secondary source identifies the place, but a current owner or city confirmation is still required.
