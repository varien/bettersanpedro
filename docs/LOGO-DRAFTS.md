# BetterSanPedro.ph Logo Drafts

Status: Draft for owner review. These are editable SVG concepts and have not replaced the current site logo.

## Options

- [Option A — River & Sun](../public/branding/logo-option-a-river-sun.svg): layered water, sunrise, and a civic blue wordmark.
- [Option B — Civic Sun](../public/branding/logo-option-b-civic-sun.svg): compact `SP` monogram with a sun and paired water lines.
- [Option C — City & Lake](../public/branding/logo-option-c-city-lake.svg): city silhouette, rising sun, and lake-inspired waves.

All three keep the Better LGU visual cues of blue, gold, a sun motif, and a strong wordmark while avoiding an official-government seal treatment. Select one direction or request a revision before it is copied into `public/icons/` and used by the Navbar, Footer, favicon, or metadata.
