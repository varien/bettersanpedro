# Migration Brief 03 — Government

## Scope

Migrate the Government landing page, City Hall directory, Sangguniang Panlungsod page, and legislative landing page from Indang to San Pedro without redesigning the site.

## Approved changes

- Replace municipal and Indang terminology with City of San Pedro terminology.
- Publish the current mayor and vice mayor from official government sources.
- Publish City Hall office locations, trunkline, and extensions from the official directory.
- Publish the Sangguniang Panlungsod Secretariat address and telephone number.
- Add visible source and freshness notes.
- Keep the full council roster, committee assignments, emergency hotlines, and national-agency contacts unpublished until current primary sources confirm them.
