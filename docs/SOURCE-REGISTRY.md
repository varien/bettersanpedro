## Verification policy

- Tier 1: city or national government primary source; publish with URL, title, publication date, and last-checked date.
- Tier 2: official provincial, barangay, or responsible-agency source; cross-check when practical.
- Tier 3: reputable secondary source; use for context only and label it.
- Verify contacts, service requirements, fees, and officials before each release or at least every 30 days.
- Recheck statistics and barangay data when the responsible agency publishes a new release; recheck tourism and history at least annually.
- If a source is unavailable or stale, show the uncertainty and do not fill the gap with an uncited claim.
